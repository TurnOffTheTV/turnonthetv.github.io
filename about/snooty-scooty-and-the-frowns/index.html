About Snooty Scooty and the Frowns

This game was originally programed on Khan Academy. When I wanted more, I moved it here (the original is still on Khan Academy, by the way).
The goal is to get to the door at the end of each level.
The music is mostly autoplay and the beat sequencer in GarageBand.

The controls are as follows:

Keyboard

Movement:

  • A and D to move
  • W to jump

Walls:

  • Jump onto a wall to slide down it.
  • W decreases slide speed, S increases it.
  • W and A or D to jump off the wall.

Menus:

  • Move mouse to select button, left click to press it.
  • Escape to pause (in-game)
  • Shift and Escape to exit pause menu

Touch:

Touch is not supported yet.

Controller:

(The button numbers can be found at https://gamepad-tester.com/)
If you use Google Chrome, your controller will vibrate when you die (if your controller supports vibration).

  • Button 16 reloads the page

Supported Controllers

Snooty Scooty and the Frowns is tested and is fully playable with:

  • DualShock 3 (PS3 Controller)
  • DualShock 4 (PS4 Controller, supports vibration)
  • DualSense (PS5 Controller)
  • Nintendo Switch Joy-Cons (supports vibration)
  • Nintendo Switch Pro Controller (supports vibration)
  • Logitech RumblePad

Movement:

  • Left stick or left and right directional buttons to move
  • Button 0 to jump

Walls:

  • Jump onto a wall to slide down it.
  • Left stick up or directional button up decreases slide speed, Left stick down or directional button down increases it.
  • Button 0 and Left stick left or right or directional button left or right to jump off the wall.

Menus:

  • Left stick or directional buttons to select button
  • Button 0 to press it
  • Button 9 to pause (in-game)
  • Button 1 to exit pause menu

Multiplayer

Play Snooty Scooty and the Frowns with up to 3 friends locally (on the same computer).

Keyboard:

  • Player 2: I to jump, J and L to move.
  • Player 3: Up directional key to jump, left and right directional keys to move.
  • Player 4: T to jump, F and H to move.

Controllers:

  • Move the stick or press the directional buttons on a controller to add a player. The order in which you activate the
    controller determines its player, first controller is first player, second controller is second player, etc.

Removing Players

In the pause menu, you can drop out any player other than player 1.
